About

This site is the result of an ongoing project at the CulturePlex Lab about the representation of the human in world painting. Your Face in History

In order to be able to provide links to the results obtained by the users, Your Face in History collects and store the images and the independent facial traits recognized into a database. Images cannot be accessible from outside the site. However, if you are unhappy with this, please, send us an email containing the URL of your result and we will remove that image as soon as possible.

Faces were extracted from the paintings stored in pintura.aut.org.
